About Us

Discover the story behind CozyCup Café

Cozy Cup was founded by two college friends who bonded over countless late-night coffee runs during their university years. They always dreamed of creating a warm, relaxing café where people could feel at home. After graduation, they reunited and turned that shared dream into reality, opening Cozy Cup as a welcoming space filled with good coffee, comfort, and friendship.
